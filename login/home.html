Welcome!

This project allows a user to sign into their Google Account, retrieve their photos and albums, and display them through this application.

Albums

Presents all users Google Photo Albums along with the title of the album and the number of photos in the album.

The user can choose if they would like to display an album by selecting the check box below the album cover.

View Albums »

Photos

Presents all users Google Photos onto multiple pages.

The user cannot choose a photo to display, but they can view their entire photo gallery.

View Photos »

Display

Presents all Google Photos that the user selected to be displayed.

Once an album has been chosen to be displayed, it will show up here until the user goes back to Albums and deselects the album.

View Display »
